# Regional Outage Runbook

## Symptoms

- One region reports high failures
- Packet loss increases
- Viewer count drops
- Streams unavailable

## Possible Causes

- ISP outage
- Regional CDN outage
- Fiber cut
- Routing issue

## Recommended Actions

1. Redirect traffic to another region.
2. Contact ISP.
3. Verify routing tables.
4. Check regional CDN health.
5. Inform operations team.

## Verification

Regional failure rate should fall below 2%.
